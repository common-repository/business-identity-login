Customize your WordPress login page with your business identity, including logo and color scheme.

== Description ==

Business Identity Login allows you to easily customize your WordPress login page to match your business branding. With this plugin, you can:

* Use your site icon or upload a custom logo
* Set a custom background color
* Choose a custom button color
* Adjust the logo aspect ratio

This plugin is perfect for businesses, agencies, and freelancers who want to provide a branded login experience for their clients or team members.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `business-identity-login` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Settings > Business Identity Login to configure your custom login page

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I use my own logo instead of the site icon? =

Yes, you can choose between using your site icon or uploading a custom logo in the plugin settings.

= What image formats are supported for the custom logo? =

The plugin supports JPG, PNG, GIF, and SVG formats for the custom logo.

= Will this plugin affect my site's performance? =

No, this plugin only affects the login page and does not impact your site's frontend performance.
